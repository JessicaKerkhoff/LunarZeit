What is LunarZeit?

LunarZeit is a beautifully crafted self-tracking app that respects your privacy. You can record your cycles, moods, symptoms, dreams, or rituals — all stored securely on your device.

What we don't do:

  • No cloud storage
  • No data harvesting
  • No third-party tracking
  • We cannot and will not provide your data to governments or anyone else — because we never see it
